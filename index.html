WELCOME TO PINK GORILLA

Our company was established in 2015 transforming what was a hobby and passion into a business. Following on from a background in precision engineering and 30 years as a police officer, Pink Gorilla Ltd based in Sedgefield, Co. Durham offers a service focused on high quality installations with excellent customer service.

Every installation is individually designed to meet with each customers specific need, we will work with you to develop your ideas at the design stage in order to assist you to create the space you desire and then deliver a complete installation service according to your own requirements.

We work in partnership with established suppliers and local businesses to offer an extensive choice of products, this combined with a meticulous eye for detail and a wealth of experience means we can work together with you to create your perfect space.

Services Provided

Kitchens

We can cater for all aspects of the fitting process from removal and disposal to plastering, plumbing, tiling, lighting and the expert fitting of units and worktops.

Bathrooms

We offer a full bathroom design and makeover service, including the installation of suite, fitted units, plastering, tiling, upvc cladding and ceilings, heated towel rails, coving and lighting.

Bedrooms

We provide fitted bedroom solutions for our customers, including layout and design, followed by installation of standard or bespoke bedroom furniture in your home.

Gardens

Whether you want to transform your garden into a sanctuary or an entertaining area, utilise our experience and expertise to create imaginative and visually exciting spaces for you to enjoy.

Interior Finishing

From bespoke radiator cabinets to wall mounted TVs and general joinery work, Pink Gorilla has it covered.
